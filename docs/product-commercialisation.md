# Nexus Product & Commercialisation Plan

## Category

Nexus should be positioned as an Event Reconstruction Intelligence platform.

It turns fragmented digital material into timelines, entities, clusters, contradictions, risks and source-linked narrative.

## Core product promise

```text
Understand what actually happened from chaotic digital evidence.
```

## Primary markets

### 1. Personal archive reconstruction

Users with huge personal archives, screenshots, exports, phone data, social media exports and PDFs who need to recover meaning.

Indicative pricing:

- £15/month basic
- £29/month pro
- £99 one-off archive reconstruction pack

### 2. Legal and litigation support

Solicitors, litigants in person, civil claims, employment disputes, family disputes, police complaint work, safeguarding chronologies.

Indicative pricing:

- £99–£499 per case workspace
- £49–£199/month professional plans
- paid report exports

### 3. Investigative / OSINT users

Journalists, private investigators, researchers and campaigners.

Indicative pricing:

- £30–£150/month
- team workspaces
- source-linked graph exports

### 4. Enterprise / compliance

Internal investigations, safeguarding reviews, HR investigations, misconduct reviews, audit trails.

Indicative pricing:

- £5,000–£50,000+/year depending on seats, volume and support.

## Suggested plans

### Personal

- 1 user
- limited storage
- screenshot and PDF ingestion
- basic graph, timeline and search

### Pro

- larger storage
- semantic search
- contradiction detection
- report export
- advanced clustering

### Team

- multi-user workspace
- tenant-isolated data
- role-based access
- audit logs
- shared case workspaces

### Enterprise

- SSO
- custom retention
- private deployment
- support and onboarding
- compliance controls

## Non-negotiables before selling

- tenant isolation;
- authentication;
- audit log;
- terms/privacy policy;
- upload limits;
- deletion/export rights;
- clear disclaimer: Nexus assists analysis; it does not determine legal truth.

## Positioning lines

```text
Nexus — Reconstruct what happened.
Nexus — Turn digital chaos into evidence-backed understanding.
Nexus — Find the forgotten context inside your archive.
Nexus — From screenshots, exports and PDFs to timelines, networks and answers.
```

## Landing page structure

1. Hero: Reconstruct what happened from digital chaos.
2. Problem: Screenshots, PDFs, exports and messages lose meaning over time.
3. Solution: Nexus ingests, links and explains.
4. Use cases: personal, legal, investigative, organisational.
5. Trust: source trace, tenant isolation, audit log.
6. Call to action: upload a test archive / request early access.

## Revenue realism

Small personal SaaS can reach £2k–£10k/month if the niche is reached.

Professional/legal niche can support £10k–£50k/month with fewer customers if trust and output quality are high.

Enterprise requires sales, security controls and support, but can become six figures per year with the right buyer.
